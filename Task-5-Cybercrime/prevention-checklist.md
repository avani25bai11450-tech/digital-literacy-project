## Stay Safe Online – Checklist
# General Safety Tips-
Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments in emails, messages, or social media.
Verify the authenticity of websites before entering personal or login details.
Do not share OTP, passwords, or personal information with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or official source.
Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates and antivirus software.
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
Log out from accounts when using shared or public devices.
Be cautious of fake profiles and friend requests on social media.
# UPI / Financial Safety Tips-
Always verify the receiver’s details before making any UPI or online payment.
Do not share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances.
# In Case of Fraud-
Immediately report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance
