# Social Media Checklist

## Do’s
- Think before you post anything online
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone
- Protect your personal information and privacy
- Verify information before sharing
- Use strong passwords and security settings

## Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details publicly
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or arguments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not overshare or post impulsively
